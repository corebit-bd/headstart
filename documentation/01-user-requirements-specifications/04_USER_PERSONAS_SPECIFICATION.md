# User Personas Specification

## 1. Persona 1 — The Aspiring ACCA Student

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Rafi Hossain |
| **Age** | 20 |
| **Role on Platform** | General / Public User (Pre-Registration) |
| **Location** | Dhaka, Bangladesh |
| **Education** | Completed A-Levels / HSC |
| **Device Usage** | Primarily Smartphone (Android); Laptop for Study |
| **Tech Comfort** | Moderate — Comfortable with Social Media & Mobile Applications |

### 1.1 Background

Rafi has just completed his HSC and is exploring career options. His father works in finance and has recommended ACCA. He has heard of HeadStart through a Facebook post shared by a friend. He visits the website to understand what ACCA is, what levels HeadStart teaches and how much it costs before deciding to enroll.

### 1.2 Goals

- Understand the ACCA Pathway and which level to start at
- See proof that HeadStart produces successful students (Prize Winners, Alumni Outcomes)
- Find out fees and how to pay
- Contact HeadStart easily if he has questions

### 1.3 Pain Points

- Overwhelmed by ACCA's Complexity — Need to have a simple explanation
- Uncertain whether the institution is credible and accredited
- Worried about paying online (unfamiliar with International Payment Gateways)

### 1.4 Key Quote

> *"I just want to know if this place is legit, what I'll learn and whether I can afford it."*
