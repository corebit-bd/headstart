## Basic Design Specification

### 🎨 Style Guide Specification

HeadStart operates as a Dual-Wing Platform (Academic & Advisory). The Design Language is "**Professional Academic Excellence**" — merging the prestige of a GOLD Approved Learning Partner with the modern, data-driven feel of a top-tier Advisory Firm.

- **Core Values** : Excellence, Professionalism, Ethics, Integrity.
- **​Visual Strategy** : Clean Layouts, High-Contrast Typography & a "SaaS-lite" Dashboard Feel to ensure Students (like Aisha) & Administrators (like Michael) can navigate complex Financial Data with ease.

### Typography System Specification

Typography is optimized for long-form Study & Financial Reporting Accuracy.

- **Headings** : **Montserrat** Font
  - **Weight** :
    - **Bold (700)** : Used for `H1` & `H2` HTML Tag Elements.
    - **Medium (500)** : Used for `H3` & `H4` HTML Tag Elements.

  - **Use Case** : Used for High-Level Brand Messaging & Navigation.

- **Body & Data** : **Inter** Font
  - **Weight** :
    - **Regular (400)** : Used for Paragraphs.
    - **Semi-Bold (600)** : Used for Data Values.

  - **Use Case** : Used for Course Materials, Student records & Financial Reports.
