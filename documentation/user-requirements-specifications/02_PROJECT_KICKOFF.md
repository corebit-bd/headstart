## Project Kick-Off ⚽️

### Project Vision 🚀

**HeadStart** aims to establish itself as a premier provider of ACCA courses, delivering an engaging & seamless learning experience for students. To achieve this, a Website & an E-Platform need to be developed that cater to four primary user types : Students, Teachers, Staff & Administrators. The platform will integrate essential Learning Management System (LMS) functionalities, ensuring smooth course enrollment, resource management, student-teacher interaction & administrative efficiency.

### Purpose

- **Enhance Accessibility** : Provide a centralized platform for ACCA Students, Faculty & Staff to interact & access resources efficiently.
- **Improve Student Experience** : Develop an interactive Student Portal where students can enroll in courses, manage learning materials & engage in discussions.
- **Facilitate Teacher-Student Engagement** : Enable Teachers to manage their courses, interact with Students & share education materials seamlessly.
- **Strengthen Administrative Capabilities** : Equip Staff & Administrators with tools to oversee academic activities, manage faculty & student records & monitor the institution’s financial performance.
- **Increase Visibility & Outreach** : Offer a modern, user-friendly website with comprehensive information on courses, faculty, events & students success stories.
- **Integrate Payment Solutions** : Provide secure & efficient payment processing for course enrollments.
- **Enhance Communication** : Implement a 24-Hour Forum & Blog System for Community Discussions & Academic Support.

### Overview Statement

The **HeadStart** Website & E-Platform will serve as a comprehensive ecosystem that streamlines academic operations, fosters engagement among students and faculty, and ensures efficient administrative management. Through a modern and user-friendly platform, HeadStart will continue to empower ACCA students and professionals, solidifying its reputation as a leading ACCA course provider.

### Business Model

The Business Model of HeadStart is designed to generate revenue while providing a high quality learning experience for ACCA students. The model incorporates multiple revenue streams, cost structures & value propositions to ensure sustainability & growth.

1. **Revenue Streams** : HeadStart will generate revenue through the following streams :
   - **Course Enrollment Fees** :
     - Students will pay a fee to enroll in ACCA courses.
     - Different pricing tiers based on course levels, bundles or premium content access.
   - **Subscription Model for LMS Access** : A recurring monthly / annual subscription for students who want continuous access to course materials, quizzes, assignments & interactive learning features.

   - **Premium Learning Features** : Students can purchase additional learning resources, mock exams, 1-on-1 mentorship sessions or personalized study plans for an extra fee.

   - **Corporate Training & B2B Partnerships** :
     - Partner will corporate firms to offer ACCA training programs for employees.
     - Custom Corporate Training Solutions for Finance & Accounting Professionals.

   - **Advertisement & Sponsorship** :
     - Monetize Blog Pages by allowing industry-related sponsors & partners to promote relevant products or services.
     - Feature sponsored content related to ACCA Exam Preparation, Finance & Career Growth.

   - **Digital Products & Resource Sales** :
     - Selling E-Books, Past Exam Papers, Recorded Lectures & Study Guides as standalone purchases.
     - Offering paid access to premium webinars & masterclasses by ACCA Professionals.

   - **Certification & Accreditation Services** : Provide certification upon course completion with an optional accreditation fee for industry-recognized certificates.

2. **Cost Structure** : HeadStart will incur costs in the following areas :
   - **Technology & InfraStructure** :
     - Development & Maintenance of the Website, Web Application & Mobile-Friendly LMS.
     - Hosting, Cloud Storage & Security InfraStructure for Data Protection.

   - **Course Content Development** :
     - Creating High-Quality ACCA Course Materials, Recorded Lectures & Study Resources.
     - Hiring ACCA Experts & Faculty Members for Content Development.

   - **Marketing & Customer Acquisition** :
     - Digital marketing campaigns (SEO, Social Media, Google Ads).
     - Partnering with influencers & ACCA Alumni for promotions.
     - Organizing webinars & free demo classes to attract new students.

   - **Faculty & Staff Salaries** :
     - Paying teachers, administrators & staff involved in managing operations & providing academic support.

   - **Customer Support & Student Engagement** :
     - Maintaining a support team for student inquiries, technical issues & course guidance.
     - Investing in Chatbots, Live Chat Support & Community Management.

   - **Legal & Compliance** :
     - Ensuring Compliance with Educational Regulations, Data Protection Laws & Payment Security Standards.

3. **Value Proposition** : HeadStart differentiates itself through :
   - **High-Quality ACCA Education** : Offering structured, exam oriented courses taught by industry experts.

   - **Flexible Learning** : Providing self-paced & instructor-led courses with live Q&A sessions.

   - **Community & Support** : Enabling students to interact through forums, blogs & peer discussions.

   - **Technology-Driven Learning** : Leveraging AI-based analytics for personalized learning paths.

   - **Career Advancement** : Helping students prepare for ACCA exams & guiding them toward job opportunities.

4. **Customer Segments** : HeadStart targets multiple customer segments :
   - **Students** : Individuals preparing for ACCA Certification.

   - **Working Professionals** : Accountants & Financial Professionals seeking Career Advancement.

   - **Corporates & Firms** : Businesses investing in employee training for financial certifications.

   - **Educational Institutions** : Universities / colleges looking to integrate ACCA into their curriculum.

5. **Key Partnerships** : To scale operations & enhance offerings, HeadStart will collaborate with :
   - **ACCA Associations & Certifications Bodies** : Ensuring credibility & recognition.

   - **Tech Providers** : Cloud Hosting, LMS Software & CyberSecurity Firms.

   - **Financial Institutions** : Payment Gateways for seamless transactions.

   - **Industry Experts & Tutors** : Recruiting top educators for content development & mentorship.

The Business Model of HeadStart is designed to create a profitable & scalable education platform by combining direct course fees, subscription models, corporate partnerships & digital product sales. By leveraging technology, community engagement & quality content, HeadStart aims to establish itself as the leading ACCA Course Provider.

### Keywords / Terms

1. **ERP** : Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is a module for streamlining administrative tasks such as billing, payroll, staff management & resource allocation. The list of features included in the ERP is discussed in the section [High-Level Features List](#high-level-features-list).

2. **CRM** : Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a module for managing relationships with students & clients focused on improving communication and satisfaction. The list of features included in the CRM is discussed in the section [High-Level Features List](#high-level-features-list).

3. **SCM** : Supply Chain Management (SCM) is a module to manage procurement, inventory & logistics of educational materials & physical assets (like furniture & classroom equipment). The list of features included in the SCM is discussed in the section [High-Level Features List](#high-level-features-list).

4. **Payment Gateway** : The online system for processing student payments. It will be done with SSL Commerz.

5. **Role-Based Access Control (RBA)** : A security mechanism ensuring different levels of access for different users.

6. **Financial Monitoring** : A feature tracking revenue, expenses & profitability.

7. **HeadStart Portal (HSP)** : HeadStart Portal (HSP), also known as the E-Platform for different user roles (students, teachers, staff & administrators).

8. **Learning Management System (LMS)** : The system that manages online courses, enrollment, assignments & learning resources.

9. **Dashboard** : A personalized user interface displaying relevant tools & data for each role.

10. **Notice Board** : A centralized space for important announcements & updates.

11. **Forum** : An interactive discussion space for students, teachers & staff.

12. **Blog** : A platform for users to post educational articles, news & insights.

13. **Student Portal** : The web application interface inside HeadStart Portal (HSP) where student manage their enrolled courses, assignments, quizzes & payments.

14. **Teacher Portal** : The web application interface inside HeadStart Portal (HSP) for instructors / faculty members to create & manage courses.

15. **Staff Portal** : The web application interface inside HeadStart Portal (HSP) where HeadStart staff oversee operations & communications.

16. **Administrator Portal** : The highest access level for managing the platform, users & analytics.

17. **Enrollment** : The process of registering for an ACCA Course.

18. **Quizzes & Assignments** : Interactive assessments for students.

19. **Course Materials** : Learning resources such as videos, PDFs & notes.

20. **Student Progress Tracking** : A feature allowing teachers & students to monitor academic performance.

21. **Analytics & Reporting** : Tools for generating insights & reports on operational & financial performance.

22. **CRUD** : Create-Read-Update-Delete Access to information.

23. **Weekly Newsletter** : Users (General / Public, Students, Teachers, Staff) who subscribe to HeadStart Weekly Newsletter will get updates about HeadStart every week in their emails.

24. **User Registration** : All Types of Users will register from the Website with Automated Unique Code Generated. _Example_ : `HS-STD21077` for a particular HeadStart Student.

25. **MCQ** : Multiple-Choice Questions will be available after covering a lecture in each module. Students need to answer MCQ to access the next module. Teacher will create MCQs for each lecture.

26. **Practice Platform** : Topic-based Past Year Questions & Solutions where students can practice past test papers & prepare for exams.

27. **Security** : Using **OTP & CAPTCHA** during login. This feature is not applicable for Administrators.

28. **Profile Lock Mechanism** : More than 2 unsuccessful login attempts will lead to Profile Lock i.e. Incorrect OTP Code Entry.

29. **SASM** : Single Active Session Management (SASM) will allow one User (Students / Teachers / Staff of HeadStart / Administrator) to login from one single device at a time.

These keywords / terms are essential for understanding & communicating effectively with stakeholders during the development, implementation & operation of the Website and E-Platform of HeadStart.

### Project Scope

The **HeadStart Website & E-Platform** aims to provide a seamless learning experience for ACCA students while streamlining administrative, teaching & financial operations. The project scope defines the functionalities, deliverables, limitations & key integrations necessary for successful development.

#### In-Scope

1. **Public Website** : For General Users & Visitors. Menu Links & Pages for HeadStart Website are as follows :
   - **About Us** :Information about HeadStart, its Mission & Vision.
   - **Services** : List of Services provided by HeadStart.
     - **ACCA Courses** : List of ACCA Academic Courses.
     - **Advisory**
     - **Trainings**
     - **Students Development Center (SDC)**
   - **Enroll Now** : Login / Signup Page for Students, Teachers & Staff.
   - **Blog** : Blog Page will have multiple sections as follows :
     - Students' Voice / Testimonials
     - ACCA Champs
     - Events
     - Success Stories
     - New Joiners
   - **Faculty** : List of Faculty Members.
   - **Contact Us** : A Contact Form for Inquiries.

2. **HeadStart Portal / E-Platform** : For Registered Users (Students, Teachers, Staff Members & Administrators). Each type of user will have role-specific features :
   - **Student Portal** :
     - View, Enroll & Manage Courses.
     - Access & Submit Assignments, Quizzes & Exams.
     - Engage in a **24/7 Interactive Forum** (View, Create, Edit & Delete Posts). Can also add comments, likes, share posts & replies to posts.
     - Upload & manage learning **resources** (documents, images, videos, PDFs).
     - View the **Notice Board** for organizational updates.
     - Manage personal **blog posts**.
     - Secure **payment integration** for course enrollment.

   - **Teacher Portal** :
     - Create & Manage Assigned Courses.
     - View Enrolled Students.
     - Track Student Progress & Performance.
     - Upload & Manage Course Resources.
     - Participate in & Moderate the **Forum**.
     - Post Updates on the **Notice Board**.
     - Manage Personal **Blog Posts**.

   - **Staff Portal** :
     - View & Manage Students & Teachers Records.
     - Manage & post notices on the **Notice Board**.
     - Participate in & oversee the **Forum**.

   - **Administrator Portal** :
     - Manage **Students, Teachers & Staff** records.
     - Oversee Course Creation & Modifications.
     - Manage **Blog** Content, **Notice Board** & **Forum** Moderation.
     - **Track Financial Performance** : Revenue, Expenses & Profit Reports.
     - Access & Manage **ERP, CRM & SCM** Systems.

3. **Target Users & their Functionalities** :
   - **General / Public Users (Website Visitors)** :
     - Access information about HeadStart & its ACCA Courses & other Services.
     - Explore Faculty Profiles & Contact Details.
     - Read Blogs featuring Testimonials, Events & Success Stories.
     - Use a Contact Form for Inquiries.
     - Register / Login for (Student / Teacher / Staff / Administrator) Access.

   - **Student** :
     - View & manage enrolled courses.
     - Enroll in new courses.
     - Manage personal blogs.
     - Access the Notice Board of HeadStart (View Access Only).
     - Engage in a 24/7 interactive forum with full CRUD Access Functionality.
     - Upload & manage learning resources.
     - Make payments for courses through integrated payment processing.
     - Take quizzes & complete assignments for courses.

   - **Teacher** :
     - Create courses.
     - View assigned courses & enrolled students.
     - Manage personal blogs.
     - Access the Notice Board of HeadStart (View Access Only).
     - Participate in & moderate a 24/7 interactive forum.
     - Upload & manage teaching resources.
     - Interact with students through course discussions & forums.
     - Track the academic performance of students for each courses.

   - **Staff** :
     - View & manage student & teachers list.
     - Manage & Post Notices on the Notice Board of HeadStart.
     - Participate in a 24/7 interactive forum with full access.
   - **Administrator** :
     - View & manage the list of students, teachers & staff.
     - Oversee & manage the courses, classrooms & blog contents of HeadStart.
     - Manage the Notice Board of HeadStart.
     - Monitor financial performance through revenue, expense & profit reports.
     - Participate in & moderate the 24/7 interactive forum.
     - Can view & manage ERP, CRM & SCM of HeadStart.

- 3rd-Party Integrations beyond the defined scope (_Example_ : AI-Driven Tutors, Blockchain Credentials).
