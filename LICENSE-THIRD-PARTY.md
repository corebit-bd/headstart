## License Compatibility

### Open Source Integrity

All Licenses used in the HeadStart Project are compatible with the **MIT License** of the Main Codebase & do not impose viral Copyleft Requirements.

### LGPL Exception (`psycopg2`)

The `psycopg2` Library (used for PostgreSQL Connectivity) is licensed under LGPL with a special Exception that allows its use in Proprietary Applications when used as a Library. This ensures that the HeadStart Proprietary Business Logic remains protected.

### Commercial Usage

All Dependencies are verified as safe for Commercial Use. This is critical for the **Advisory Wing** Operations, ensuring that the Platform can facilitate Paid Consultancy Services (BPO, Audit, Tax) without licensing friction.
